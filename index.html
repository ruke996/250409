Overview 2024 the International Conference on Digital Contents: AICoM(AI, IoT, Contents and Metaverse) Technologies (DigiCon-24) will be held in Jeju, Korea, December 16 ~ December 18, 2024. DigiCon-24 will be the eighth event of DigiCon conference series which is hosted and organized by the Korea Digital Contents Society (K-DCS) and the Institution of Creative Research Professionals (ICRP). Digital contents have become increasingly important not only in the content industry but also in our daily lives. They are being utilized in various cultural fields including entertainment, education, arts, humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and engineering. Today, creative digital content technologies in smart media, arts, and culture are leading the way to a new world, and we are living in an era of content platforms where content consumers can instantly become content producers. Now, digital contents and its major fields including smart media, arts and culture technology are widely recognized as important academic disciplines, and the number of researchers studying these topics is rapidly increasing. All fields are entering, connecting with, or converging with the digital contents field. The DigiCon-24 international conference is an excellent opportunity to discuss and explore areas related to the [Digital Contents: AICoM(AI, IoT, Contents and Metaverse) Technologies] with fellow students, researchers, and practitioners from academia and industry working in all areas of Digital Contents Technology and its Applications.
